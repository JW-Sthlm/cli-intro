# Demo 02 — Partner Work with PMX: Preparing for a Connect

> **Duration:** ~10 min  
> **Goal:** Walk through a realistic partner Connect preparation using PMX, MSX, M365, and screenshot tools — all from Copilot CLI

---

## Setup

- Copilot CLI running with PMX, M365, and GitHub MCP servers loaded
- Pick a real partner you have projects with (or use a well-known one for demo purposes)
- Have an email or Teams chat related to that partner visible

---

## Scenario

*"You have a partner Connect meeting tomorrow. You need to prepare: review your projects, check outcomes, look at opportunities, and draft talking points."*

---

## Script

### 1. Pull your active projects

**Type:**

```
Show me my active PMX projects
```

**Talking point:** This hits Dynamics 365 live. No need to open a browser, navigate to PMX, filter views. Just ask.

---

### 2. Check outcome coverage

**Type:**

```
Which of my projects are missing outcomes?
```

**Talking point:** This is one of the most common PMX hygiene issues. Instead of clicking through each project, Copilot checks them all at once. Point out: MSX Opportunity, Solution ID, Referral — the three outcome types.

---

### 3. Dive into a specific partner

**Type:**

```
Show me the team for [Partner Name] — who is PDM, PTS, PSAM?
```

**Talking point:** Before a Connect, you want to know who's in what role. This pulls the partner team directly.

---

### 4. Search for related opportunities

**Type:**

```
Search MSX opportunities for [Partner Name] — show me open ones in EMEA
```

**Talking point:** Now we're crossing from PMX into MSX. Copilot connects the dots between your project work and the sales pipeline. Look for gaps — opportunities without projects, or projects without linked opportunities.

---

### 5. Screenshot context

**Open a partner email or Teams chat on screen. Take a screenshot.**

**Type:**

```
Look at my last screenshot. What is the key ask or topic in this message?
```

**Talking point:** Sometimes the context you need is visual — an email you received, a dashboard, a chat thread. Screenshot it, ask Copilot to summarize.

---

### 6. Draft talking points

**Type:**

```
Based on the PMX projects, MSX opportunities, and partner team you just showed me — draft 3-4 talking points for my Connect meeting with [Partner Name] tomorrow. Focus on: outcome gaps, active opportunities, and any projects that need attention.
```

**Talking point:** This is the payoff. Everything we just looked at — Copilot synthesizes into actionable talking points. You could paste this into your meeting notes or email it to the partner team.

---

### 7. (Optional) Draft a prep email

**Type:**

```
Draft a short email to the PDM for [Partner Name] with the talking points we just created. Use a professional but informal tone.
```

**Talking point:** You can go from data → analysis → action in one session. No tab-switching, no copy-pasting between apps.

---

## Key Messages

- PMX + MSX + M365 = a complete Connect prep workflow
- Copilot connects data sources that you'd normally have to jump between manually
- The screenshot tool adds visual context that's hard to get otherwise
- The draft output is a starting point, not a final product — you're the curator
